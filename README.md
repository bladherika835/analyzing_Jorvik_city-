# Analyzing Jorvik City 🏙️

Investigation into why players only stay for 30 minutes.

### Key Observations:
* **Focus:** Mainly window shopping for clothes & gear.
* **Issue:** Lack of social activities and riding space.
* **Goal:** Data-driven suggestions for better player retention.




## Comparison: City vs. Island

| Location | Horse Allowed | Avg. Stay | Key Activity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jorvik City** | ❌ No | 30 min | Shopping |
| **Jorvik Island**| ✅ Yes | 120 min | Racing & Socializing |

**Conclusion:** The presence of horses and social training spots is the main driver for player retention.
